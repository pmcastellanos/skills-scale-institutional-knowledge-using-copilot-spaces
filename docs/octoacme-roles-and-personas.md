# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## UX Designers

### Role Summary
UX Designers create intuitive, accessible, and delightful user experiences. They conduct user research, design interfaces, and validate designs with users to ensure products meet usability standards and customer needs.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Create wireframes, mockups, and interactive prototypes
- Define user flows and interaction patterns
- Collaborate with Product Managers on user stories and acceptance criteria
- Work with Developers to ensure design feasibility and implementation fidelity
- Maintain design systems and style guides

### Goals
- Maximize user satisfaction and task completion rates
- Reduce friction and cognitive load in user interfaces
- Ensure accessibility and inclusive design practices
- Validate designs with real user feedback

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews and critique sessions with Product and Engineering
- User research findings and usability test reports
- Design specifications and handoff documentation
- Collaboration in sprint planning to clarify design requirements

---

## DevOps/SRE Engineers

### Role Summary
DevOps/SRE Engineers ensure reliable, scalable, and efficient infrastructure and deployment pipelines. They bridge development and operations, automate processes, and maintain system observability and performance.

### Responsibilities
- Design and maintain CI/CD pipelines
- Monitor system health, performance, and reliability
- Implement infrastructure as code and automation
- Manage deployments, rollbacks, and incident response
- Set up logging, alerting, and observability tools
- Collaborate with Developers on deployment strategies and reliability improvements

### Goals
- Minimize deployment risk and downtime
- Improve deployment frequency and lead time
- Maintain high system availability and performance
- Reduce toil through automation

### Typical Communication
- Pre-deployment reviews and release coordination
- Incident postmortems and blameless retrospectives
- Infrastructure and architecture design reviews
- Weekly sync with PM and engineering on deployment readiness

---

## Technical Writers

### Role Summary
Technical Writers create clear, accurate, and user-focused documentation that helps users and developers understand and effectively use products and systems.

### Responsibilities
- Write and maintain user guides, API documentation, and tutorials
- Collaborate with Product and Engineering to understand features
- Review product changes for documentation impact
- Ensure documentation accuracy and consistency
- Organize and structure information for discoverability
- Gather feedback and improve documentation based on user needs

### Goals
- Reduce support burden through comprehensive documentation
- Improve time-to-productivity for new users and developers
- Maintain documentation that stays current with product changes
- Ensure documentation is accessible and easy to understand

### Typical Communication
- Sprint planning to identify documentation needs
- Reviews of feature specs and PRs for documentation impacts
- Collaboration with Support on frequently asked questions
- Release notes and change log contributions

---

## Business Analysts

### Role Summary
Business Analysts bridge business needs and technical solutions. They gather requirements, analyze business processes, and ensure solutions deliver measurable business value.

### Responsibilities
- Elicit and document business requirements
- Analyze current processes and identify improvement opportunities
- Define and validate acceptance criteria with stakeholders
- Create process flows, data models, and requirement specifications
- Facilitate communication between business stakeholders and technical teams
- Track and report on business metrics and KPIs

### Goals
- Ensure solutions align with business objectives
- Reduce ambiguity and rework through clear requirements
- Maximize return on investment for initiatives
- Enable data-driven decision making

### Typical Communication
- Requirements gathering sessions with stakeholders
- Sprint planning to clarify and prioritize backlog items
- Business case presentations and ROI analysis
- Weekly status updates on requirement clarifications and changes

---

## Support Leads

### Role Summary
Support Leads manage customer support operations and serve as the voice of the customer within the organization. They triage issues, identify trends, and advocate for improvements based on customer feedback.

### Responsibilities
- Manage support ticket queue and escalations
- Identify and communicate product issues and improvement opportunities
- Collaborate with Product and Engineering on bug prioritization
- Provide customer feedback and usage insights
- Create and maintain support knowledge base
- Train support team on new features and changes

### Goals
- Minimize customer response and resolution times
- Reduce recurring support issues through product improvements
- Improve customer satisfaction and retention
- Enable support team with effective tools and knowledge

### Typical Communication
- Daily triage of critical customer issues
- Weekly sync with Product and PM on trends and escalations
- Input on release planning for customer impact assessment
- Retrospectives to share customer feedback and improvement ideas
